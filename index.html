About Us

Our Mission

For healthcare institutions that are dissatisfied with the outdated, complex and manual way of wound care management. Curiato’s Ceylon System is a combination of a smart mattress overlay and patient management interface that provides monitoring of biological factors of high acuity patients, aimed towards decreasing nurse fatigue, and simplifying communication between care staff and providers.

Our system provides:

  • Real Time Visualization: Localized body mapping of anatomical positions through individualized deep learning models for continuous personalization of care plans.
  • Digital Workflows: Digitization of institutional specific prevention workflows accessible from multi device streamlining processes and improving communication.
  • Seamless Integration: Have the ability of incorporating already existing scales and protocols for enhancement of workflow efficiency.
  • Data interoperability: Connect, store, and share data the way you want. Leveraging our open API and fog computing through the Ceylon in-bed computer and cloud computing service.
  • Actionable insights: Timely preventative recommendations to take actions through insights from analyzing collected data through the deep learning models and workflow process modeling.
  • Data Usability: Take advantage of actionable data that supports evidence and value based care.
